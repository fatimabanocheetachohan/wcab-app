# WCAB – Local Ride Booking Platform

WCAB is a local ride booking and driver–customer connecting platform.

This application helps passengers connect directly with nearby active drivers.
WCAB does NOT provide transportation services and does NOT act as a cab operator.

---

## How WCAB Works
- Users can see a list of nearby active drivers
- Passengers directly contact drivers by call or message
- Fare is decided mutually between driver and passenger
- WCAB does not fix prices and does not take commission

---

## Important Disclaimer
- WCAB is only a connecting platform
- All drivers are independent and self-employed
- WCAB is NOT responsible for:
  - rides
  - payments
  - disputes
  - accidents
  - losses or damages
- Any agreement is strictly between driver and passenger

---

## Privacy
- WCAB does not sell or share user data with third parties
- Information is used only to connect drivers and passengers
